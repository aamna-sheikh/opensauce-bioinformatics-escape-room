Bioinformaticians use BLAST (Basic Local Alignment Search Tool) to compare an unknown biological sequence against large databases of known sequences.

BLAST searches for similar sequences and ranks the results based on how closely they match. A strong match can help scientists determine what a protein is, predict its biological function, and understand where it may have come from.
